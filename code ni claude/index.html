About Farm2Door

Our story, our mission, and the people behind every harvest

Our Story

How Farm2Door Began

Farm2Door was founded on a simple belief: every family deserves access to fresh, safe, and affordable food — and every farmer deserves a fair price for their hard work. We started as a small community project connecting a few local farms with neighborhood families.

Today, we partner with over 50 local farms across the region, offering more than 20 fresh product lines ranging from vegetables and fruits to grains and artisan goods. Our platform is built on trust, transparency, and a genuine love for agriculture.

We are not just an online store — we are a community that believes in the power of farm-to-table living.

🎯 Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

What We Do Every Day

Deliver fresh, safe, and affordable farm products directly to consumers while supporting local farmers. We eliminate middlemen, ensure fair pricing, and build a sustainable supply chain that benefits both producers and consumers.

🔭 Our Vision

Where We're Headed

To be the most trusted farm-to-table platform in the region, promoting sustainable agriculture, empowering Filipino farming families, and building a healthier, more food-secure community for generations to come.
